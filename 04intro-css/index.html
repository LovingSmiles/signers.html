Why Swimming Matters

People of all ages can take advantage of swimming and learn to clear the mind through swimming, easing away overthinking and daily stress.

History of Swimming

The history behind swimming goes back to the very beginning of humanity. It has been a way to get everything from food to exercise, keeping the body and mind in healthy shape for long lives. Records show swimming was practiced as far back as 2500 BCE. It was used for leisure in Egypt and for military and education in Greece and Rome.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show that no matter your swimming level, you can always improve and never stop learning. Exercise helps clear and realign the mind, body, and soul, ultimately helping you find inner peace again.
