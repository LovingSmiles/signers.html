Helpful Tools

  • Kickboard: Strengthen your legs and build balance in the water.
  • Goggles: Keep your eyes comfortable and focused underwater.
  • Pull Buoy: Improve upper body technique and endurance.
  • Swim Cap: Keep hair out of your face and reduce drag.

Training Focus

Our sessions help swimmers of all levels, from beginners learning to float, to experienced swimmers building endurance and improving speed. Each tool plays a role in perfecting your technique and helping you stay motivated.
