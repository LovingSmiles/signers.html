Thomas Jefferson

Jefferson wrote the Declaration and served as the 3rd U.S. President. His words inspired generations seeking freedom.

John Hancock

Known for his large signature, Hancock symbolized bold leadership and defiance against tyranny.

Benjamin Franklin

Inventor, diplomat, and philosopher, Franklin’s influence extended far beyond politics — into science, literature, and innovation.
